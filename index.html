Why use Expenses?

Expenses is a clean, ad-free, and distributed expense manager designed to help you organize group spending in one place.

⚡

Real-Time Sync

Everyone in the group stays updated instantly. Add an expense on your phone, and it appears on everyone else’s device in real-time.

⚖️

Simple 'Settle Up' Logic

Our smart engine calculates the net balance for everyone. No more complex back-and-forth; just see exactly who you owe or who owes you.

🔒

Secure & Private

Login securely with Google. Your data is your own, protected by cloud-grade security.

📊

Detailed Insights

View your spending habits with built-in charts. Categorize your trips and manage multiple groups simultaneously.

🚀

Lightweight & Fast

Built for performance. Access your trip dashboard, manage members, and track transfers with a zero-lag interface.

Key Features

  • Create multiple trips or groups (Home, Vacation, Dinner, etc.).
  • Add participants via unique usernames.
  • Support for unequal splits and money transfers.

  • Beautiful Material Design interface.
