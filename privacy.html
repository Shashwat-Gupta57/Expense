Privacy Policy for Expenses

Last updated: June 2026

Expenses ("the App") is developed and operated by Shashwat Gupta. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Expenses mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Expenses collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality.

Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, the following information may be collected:

  • Email address

This information is used to authenticate your account and associate your data with your profile.

User-Generated Data

The app stores information that you create while using the service, including:

  • Username
  • Trip or group names
  • Expense entries
  • Payment history
  • Participant usernames
  • Calculated balances between users

This information is required for the app to calculate and display shared expenses.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide the features of the app, including:

  • Authenticating users via Google Sign-In
  • Synchronizing expense data in real time
  • Calculating shared balances between participants
  • Displaying trip expenses to members of the same trip
  • Maintaining persistent records of expenses

Expenses does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

Expenses does not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

However, information related to trips (such as expenses, balances, and usernames) is visible to other participants within the same trip or group. This is necessary for the app’s core functionality of tracking shared expenses.

Only users added to a trip can view the data associated with that trip.

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies on trusted third-party services to function:

  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Firebase Authentication for secure login management
  • Firebase Realtime Database for storing and synchronizing app data

These services may collect limited technical information required to provide their functionality. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using Firebase cloud infrastructure, which provides industry-standard security and encryption.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including secure authentication and protected cloud storage.

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account remains active.

If you wish to request deletion of your data, you may contact us using the email provided below. Requests are typically processed within 2–5 business days.

7. Children's Privacy

Expenses is intended for users aged 13 and older.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

8. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting support.

Email: support@talken.in

Please allow 2–5 business days for processing requests.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app listing or the webpage hosting this policy.

Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
